INITIAL RELEASE:

    ChatGPT, Gemini, Tensor Art, Kling, Higgsfield.

  • Daily generation discounts (ChatGPT: 8, Gemini: 20).

  • Monthly cycle reset on day 17.
  • Movement history in history.txt (readable format).
  • Desktop notifications with plyer.
  • tkinter GUI with image gallery and video list.
  • Credits & generations table per profile.
  • Dynamic profile tabs (add/remove from UI).
  • .bat launcher for quick access.
  • Multi-language support (English, Spanish, Chinese).
  • Dark/Light theme support.
  • GitHub auto-updater (update.bat).
